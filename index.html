About Anna O’Kinsky

Anna graduated from Ann May Nursing School with an RN degree, obtained her BSN from Monmouth University, and earned her Master’s in Nursing from UMDNJ with dual specialties in primary care and psychiatry. She holds certifications as a psychiatric nurse and nurse practitioner, with over 25 years of experience in both inpatient and outpatient settings.

Certifications and training include DBT (Dialectical Behavior Therapy), NLP (Neuro-Linguistic Programming), forensic training, relapse prevention, and training with the Amen Clinic in holistic psychiatry.

Anna is passionate about forming therapeutic relationships to support emotional regulation, goal achievement, and overall well-being through a balanced, holistic treatment strategy.
